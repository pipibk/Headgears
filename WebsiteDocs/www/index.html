Is it true that Hermes drew its collection “Ndop” from the traditional Cameroonian scarf?

It is true, Hermes drew its collection «Ndop» from the traditional Cameroonian scarf Ndop. The renowned brand was notably inspired by the designs on the traditional fabric. There is also the design which represents the huts present on the traditional tissu of Jukun people from Wukari Tabara in Nigéria. All these illustrations highlight the beauty of the scarfs.
